## Getting Started

### Charging

1. Connect the USB-C cable to the charging port on the left earcup.
2. Connect the other end to a USB power source (5 V / 1 A minimum).
3. The LED indicator turns **red** while charging and **white** when fully charged.
4. Full charge time: approximately **2 hours**.

:::note
Do not use the headphones during the first full charge cycle.
:::

### Pairing via Bluetooth

1. Power on the headphones by holding the **Power button** for 2 seconds.
2. Hold the **Multifunction button** for 2 seconds until the LED flashes blue.
3. On your device, open **Bluetooth Settings** and select **Aeyron AH-1**.
4. Pairing is confirmed when the LED turns solid white and you hear a tone.

:::tip
The AH-1 can store up to **3 paired devices**. To switch devices, hold the Multifunction button for 2 seconds on a previously paired device.
:::

## LED Indicators

| LED State | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| Solid white | Powered on, connected |
| Flashing blue | Pairing mode |
| Flashing white | Connecting to last device |
| Solid red | Charging |
| Flashing red | Battery low (< 10%) |
| Off | Powered off |

## Safety Information

:::warning
Keep the headphones away from water and high humidity. This product is not waterproof.
:::

:::danger
Listening at high volumes for extended periods may cause permanent hearing damage. Keep volume at a safe level.
:::
