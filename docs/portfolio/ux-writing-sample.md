# UX Writing Sample

UX writing is where technical accuracy meets product behavior. The goal is not to sound clever. The goal is to help users understand what is happening and what to do next.

## Example: firmware update

### Before

> Error. Update failed.

### After

> The firmware update could not finish because the device lost connection. Reconnect the device, then try again.

## Why the revised copy works

- It names the problem.
- It explains the likely cause.
- It gives the next action.
- It avoids blaming the user.

## Example: destructive action

### Before

> Delete device?

### After

> Remove this device from your workspace? Historical logs will stay available, but the device will stop syncing new data.

## Writing principle

Clear UX writing answers three questions:

1. What is happening?
2. Why does it matter?
3. What can I do next?
